Conservation

The Houston Zoo, located in Houston, Texas, is home to more than 6,000 animals and focuses their efforts on connecting people to wildlife conversation. They are involved in work around the world offering protection to animals in nearly every continent. Their partnerships have been critical in leading conservation action for more than 30 different projects, which protects over 600 species globally.
