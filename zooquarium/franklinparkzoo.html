Conservation

Zoo New England is home to two zoos: the Franklin Park Zoo in Boston, Massachusetts and Stone Zoo in Stoneham, Massachusetts. Together, these zoos combine almost 100 acres of land that serves conservation efforts for dozens of species, particularly reptiles and amphibians. They put a large focus on working with native animals like the New England Cottontail, several types of box turtle, and even local wildflower populations. Zoo New England also has a program where students can help with turtle conservation by supporting hatchlings of various species.
