Conservation

Reid Park Zoo is home to over 500 animals in Tucson, Arizona. Conservation is incorporated directly into the care they provide which can be attested to by their Green Business certification in 2024. They work on a variety of conservation projects, including a cell phone recycling partnership, planning out species survival plans for responsible zoo breeding, local recycling and plastic reduction, and building “green” through the use of solar panels, recycled materials, and water conservation.
