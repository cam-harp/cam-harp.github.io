Since these are live cameras, the animals may be doing something that isn’t in front of the cameras such as eating, sleeping, or interacting in another part of their enclosure. Check back later to see if they make an appearance!

We share direct links to each zoo and aquarium’s conservation information and ways to get involved on their homepages under our DIRECTORY.

ZooQuarium Live is a stepping stone in connecting you with zoos and aquariums. If you have any questions about the animals, exhibits, or institutions themselves, please contact the zoo/aquarium directly!

If you’re not seeing a zoo or aquarium you’re familiar with, there are several reasons why this could be, but the main reason is that they don’t have live cameras available online.

ZooQuarium Live is an independent student-run project that is not affiliated with any other organization including any of the zoos, aquariums, or organizations linked on our website.

Please contact us at zooquariumlive@gmail.com to send us information about your zoo/aquarium and we’ll look into adding them!
