They center their research on working with various universities and conservation organizations to better understand how to protect aquatic life. They are a part of a network of aquariums around the United States that have outlined goals for environmental justice and wildlife sustainability. They also run several programs for conservation including the Saving Animals From Extinction (SAFE) project where they work with other zoos and aquariums to protect sea life like sea turtles, coral reefs, sea stars, and sharks.
