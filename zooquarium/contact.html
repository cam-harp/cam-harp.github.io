About Us

ZooQuarium Live was designed to be an interactive website focused on supporting zoos and aquariums who are leaders in wildlife conservation in the United States. By compiling virtual live cameras in one easy-to-use place, users can interact directly with animals across the country and get involved with conservation efforts right from their device!


ZooQuarium Live is not affiliated with any zoos, aquariums, or wildlife organizations. It is a student-created project that is independent and only provides publicly-available, summarized information from various zoo and aquarium websites. Any content made available on ZooQuarium Live belongs to its respective owners.


For more information on a specific zoo or aquarium, please visit their respective websites. If you have any questions about ZooQuarium Live, check out our FAQ or contact us at zooquariumlive@gmail.com.
