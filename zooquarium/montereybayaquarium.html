Conservation

The Monterey Bay Aquarium, located on the coast of Monterey, California, combines standard aquarium exhibits with oceanfront views. It plays a huge part in conservation for the Pacific Ocean where ecosystems right outside the aquariumâ€™s walls are supported through special programs and activism. In addition to supporting local shark and bluefin tuna populations, the Monterey Bay Aquarium has the only Sea Otter Program in the world which has been helping maintain the local sea otter population for more than three decades. They also fight against plastic pollutants in the ocean, support healthy aquaculture practices, and are on the front end of fighting against climate change.
