Conservation

Located in the curve of the Yaquina Bay in Newport, Oregon, the Oregon Coast Aquarium is home to a variety of aquatic animals, encounters, and exhibits. It is known for its educational resources and partnerships with local environmental and scientific organizations. Supporting the local Yaquina Bay is central to the Oregon Coast Aquarium’s mission on conservation where they operate a rehabilitation program for endangered marine wildlife and a dive team that tracks local ocean patterns around Oregon. Their commitments to aquatic conservation have been essential for the Pacific Northwest’s conservation efforts.
