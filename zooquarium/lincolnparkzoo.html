Conservation

The Lincoln Park Zoo is a hub for conservation and dedicated wildlife care in Chicago, Illinois. The second oldest zoo in the United States, it has been supporting global efforts for decades through hundreds of partnerships, scientific studies, and projects. Some of their core focuses are on developing ways for people to better coexist with animals, how to conserve threatened species, and what methods humans can use to support animal welfare. They are home to many zoo scientists who work tirelessly to spread awareness for animal conservation and promote sustainable environments.
